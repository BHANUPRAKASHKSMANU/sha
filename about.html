About Our Platform

Connecting BCA students through collaborative learning

GM University Davanagere - Igniting Innovation, Inspiring Transformation. This blogging platform is an initiative by the BCA department to foster knowledge sharing, technical writing, and collaborative learning among our students.

About GM University

  • 10,000+ Students across various programs
  • 350+ Experienced Faculty members
  • 67 Academic Programs including BCA, MCA, MBA
  • 57 Acres Green Vibrant Campus in Davanagere, Karnataka
  • MoU with Clark University USA and De Montfort University UK

Platform Mission

To provide BCA students a dedicated space to share technical knowledge, document their learning journey, showcase projects, and build professional portfolios that reflect GM University's commitment to academic excellence.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend: Python with Django Framework
  • Database: PostgreSQL
  • Hosting: Cloud Infrastructure
